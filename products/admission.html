Features

Our Admission Portal is designed to make the Admission Process easier than never before.

Institutional Branding

The portal will be customized with the logo and branding of your Institution for a commanding presence on your users.

Download XLS and PDF

Download Admission data in Excel and PDF format for easy reference and usage. Other formats also available.

Scheduled Admissions

Enable and disable the portal for precise control on admission dates through an easy to use interface. Also available on Mobile.

Beautiful Designs

Choose from a variety of beautiful responsive modern web designs. Sites that work on PC and mobile browsers equally well.

Automatic Backups

We take regular automated backups of site data. Your site is up as and when you need it. We guarantee 99.9% uptime on all production sites.

Simply Easy to Use

We know your students might not be tech savvy. We have designed the portal so that it is easy to use and user friendly for even the most novice users.

Easily Collect Student Data

Collect student data with required and optional fields, and add custom labels to user fields. Choose which fields to collect with default values.

Upload Student Files

Allow students to upload pictures, files and documents. You can collect student photos, marksheets, and documents in JPG, PDF and other formats.

Set Date for Verfication

You can set a date for Document Verfication or for calling the students for interview individually for each student. Students will be notified when they log in to the portal.

Login ID for Each Student

Each student will have his own Login ID and password. Student can edit his profile any time before he clicks on Final Submit. Students can also check their admission status.

Connect to Your Website

Our Academic Portal can either be used as a separate portal, or it can be linked to your existing Institutional Website, for a unified online presence. The system can be used with existing Apps.

State of the Art Security

Our apps are built with State of the Art Industry Standard Security Features, so that you can be assured that your admission process is safe, secure and transparent. SSL Certificates are free.
